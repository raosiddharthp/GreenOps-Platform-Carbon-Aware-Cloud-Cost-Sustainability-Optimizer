# Phase E – 02 Migration Roadmap

## Phased Rollout Plan

| Phase       | Timeline       | Scope                                      | Success Criteria                       |
|-------------|----------------|--------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Phase 0** | Jan 2026       | PoC in sandbox project                     | 50% carbon reduction in test workload |
| **Phase 1** | Feb–Mar 2026   | Rollout to non-prod + 2 prod projects      | 100% workloads auto-tagged, reporting live |
| **Phase 2** | Apr–Jun 2026   | Full production (all GCP projects       | ≥ 30% emissions & 40% cost reduction   |
| **Phase 3** | Jul–Dec 2026   | Multi-region + predictive optimization     | ≥ 50% total reduction, AI self-healing |

## Migration Waves

| Wave | Projects | Approach                     | Duration |
|------|----------|------------------------------|-----------|
| 1    | Sandbox  | Lift-and-shift + auto-tagging| 2 weeks   |
| 2    | Dev/Test | GreenOps policies enforced   | 4 weeks   |
| 3    | Prod     | Gradual rollout + rollback   | 8 weeks   |

## Risk & Mitigation
| Risk                         | Mitigation                               |
|------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Workload downtime            | Blue/green scheduling + canary testing    |
| Resistance to change         | Executive sponsorship + training          |
| Data accuracy                | Dual-run with legacy reports for 3 months |

**Status**: Roadmap approved — rollout starts 1 Feb 2026
